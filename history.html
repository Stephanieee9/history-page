Our Company History

BrightStar Innovations began in 2014 with a simple mission: deliver practical technology that ordinary people can actually use. Our early team embraced hands-on problem solving and continuous improvement. Those small wins became major milestones as customers spread the word. We still build with that spirit today.

Milestones

  2. 2018 — Expanded nationwide distribution
  3. 2023 — Launched BrightStar Labs initiative

Core Values

  • Relentless customer focus
  • Practical, testable innovation
